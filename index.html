Hello! I’m a first-year genetics PhD student conducting research in gene regulation at Stanford University. I am currently rotating in Polly Fordyce's Lab and am grateful to be supported by a Phi Kappa Phi Graduate Fellowship.

Here are my Google Scholar, Twitter, Github, and CV.

Current Research Interests

  • High-throughput screening of genetic regulatory networks
  • Mechanisms of chromatin state maintenance
